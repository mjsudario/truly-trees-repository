Truly Trees

About Truly Trees

We are a collaborative fundraiser run by the non-profit organization, Truly Trees with the mission of planting as many trees as we can to battle deforestation and to make a better future for all the animals and people alike. For every dollar you donate, one tree gets planted. Help us make a difference by donating in kind.

Our Projects

Over the last year, we have initiated dozens of projects all over the country in hopes of fulfilling our mission of planting as much trees as we can. In due time, we might even start a tree planting project in your area! Click on a picture to know more about each project
